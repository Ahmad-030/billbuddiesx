🔒 Privacy Policy

BillBuddiesX – by DanaTypeApps

👋 Introduction

Welcome to BillBuddiesX, a bill-splitting and group expense tracking application developed by DanaTypeApps. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how your data is handled within our app.

📱 Information We Collect

BillBuddiesX is designed to work fully offline. Here's what that means:

  • All data (groups, members, expenses) is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences.
  • We do not collect any personal information.
  • We do not require account creation or login.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, location, or any device sensors.
  • No data is ever transmitted to our servers or third parties.
✅ Your data never leaves your device. Period.

🔐 Data Storage & Security

All expense records, group names, and member information you enter are stored exclusively in your device's local storage. When you uninstall the app, all data is permanently deleted. We do not use cloud sync, analytics SDKs, or crash-reporting tools that collect personal data.

🌐 Internet Access

BillBuddiesX does not require internet access to function. The app uses no network permissions. Any internet activity shown by your system is unrelated to BillBuddiesX functionality.

👶 Children's Privacy

BillBuddiesX does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not collect any personal data from any user regardless of age, making it safe for all age groups.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app. Continued use of BillBuddiesX after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

⚖️ Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app itself.
  • Deletion: Use Settings → Reset Data to permanently delete all stored information.
  • Portability: Data resides on your device and is under your full control.
